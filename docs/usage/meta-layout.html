--- title: "pree: metadata & layouts" layout: ../_layout.html --- ## Metadata & Layouts Websites typically have multiple pages, which generally follow the same layout, and share most of their assets and metadata. Each page might also have its own unique metadata, such as its title or description. Use `pree`'s front matter and layouting to manage this shared metadata and layout.
