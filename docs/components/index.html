--- title: "pree: writing components" layout: ../_layout.html --- ## Using Components [Web components](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/API/Web_components) are a key technology making `pree` (and no-build in general) possible. You can use them to re-use logic, DOM and styling across various pages of your website. Due to their isolated nature, you can also use them on multiple websites with ease, share them with others, etc.
